What is CUET UG?

Common University Entrance Test (CUET) UG is a national-level entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate programs in central universities and other participating institutions across India.

Frequently Asked Questions

There is no limit on the number of attempts for CUET UG. Candidates can appear for the exam multiple times as long as they meet the eligibility criteria.

Coaching is not mandatory for CUET UG. With proper self-study using NCERT books, previous year papers, and mock tests, students can achieve good scores. However, coaching can provide structured guidance and doubt resolution.

CUET UG follows the government reservation policy: 15% for SC, 7.5% for ST, 27% for OBC-NCL, 10% for EWS, and 5% for PwD candidates. Each participating university may have additional reservation policies.

CUET UG difficulty level is moderate and is based on NCERT curriculum. It's generally considered easier than JEE Main but requires good preparation in language, domain subjects, and general knowledge.
