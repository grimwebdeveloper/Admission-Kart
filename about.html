Our Journey

From a small idea to helping thousands of students - here's how Admission Kart evolved into India's trusted education guidance platform.

2020

The Beginning

Founded by education enthusiasts who experienced the complexity of college admissions firsthand. Started with a simple mission: make admissions transparent and accessible for every student.

2021

First 1,000 Students

Reached our first milestone of helping 1,000 students with college admissions. Launched our comprehensive counseling services and built partnerships with top colleges across India.

2022

Platform Launch

Launched our comprehensive online platform with advanced features like college comparison, entrance exam guidance, and personalized counseling. Expanded our team of expert counselors.

2023

5,000+ Success Stories

Crossed 5,000 successful admissions and received recognition from leading educational institutions. Introduced AI-powered college matching and scholarship assistance programs.

2024

10,000+ Students & Beyond

Achieved the milestone of helping 10,000+ students with a 95% success rate. Expanded to study abroad counseling and launched our mobile app for better accessibility.
