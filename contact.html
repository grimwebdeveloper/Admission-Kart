Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and admission guidance.

How can I get admission guidance from Admission Kart?

You can get admission guidance by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or booking a consultation session. Our expert counselors will provide personalized guidance based on your academic background and career goals.

Is the counseling service free or paid?

We offer both free initial consultations and premium counseling services. The first consultation is always free, and we'll discuss our premium services if you need more detailed guidance and support throughout the admission process.

What courses and colleges do you help with?

We provide guidance for all major courses including Engineering, Medical, Management, Law, Design, and more. We work with over 10,000+ colleges across India and also provide guidance for studying abroad.

Can I book a 1-on-1 counseling session?

Yes, you can book personalized 1-on-1 counseling sessions with our expert counselors. These sessions can be conducted online or at our office, based on your preference and convenience.

How quickly will I get a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, you can call us directly or reach out via WhatsApp for immediate assistance.

Do you provide support for entrance exam preparation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive guidance for entrance exam preparation including JEE, NEET, CUET, CAT, and many other competitive exams. We offer study materials, mock tests, and strategic preparation plans.
